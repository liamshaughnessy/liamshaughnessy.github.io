About Liam

Immerse yourself in the world of violin with Liam Shaughnessy, a seasoned professional known for his exceptional teaching and captivating performances. With a rich background in chamber music, having performed works such as the quartets of Mozart, the chamber music of Shostakovich, and many others. He also has performed extensively as a solo artist the most beloved works in the classical canon, including Bach’s Six Sonatas and Partitas for solo violin, the Beethoven, Sibelius, Mendelssohn, and Mozart concertos. He also is currently entering the symphonic space, and has performed symphonies of Mahler, Brahms, the poems of Strauss and Gershwin, and the works of contemporary modern composers like Gabriella Lena Frank, and Unsuk Chin. Liam's expertise shines through, particularly in each lesson and performance, offering a unique blend of skill and artistry. He uniquely priorities having an understanding of the music history behind the repertoire being performed. Liam believes, classical music is a gateway into the lives and experiences of those of the , and essential to understand their music.
